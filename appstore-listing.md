## Description - English

Listen to radio stations from around the world in one app.

CatchWave is an internet radio app that streams live radio channels from Korea, USA, UK, Japan, Europe, and more.

Key Features:
- 40+ radio channels from around the world
- Korean broadcasters: KBS, MBC, SBS, EBS, YTN, TBS
- International stations: BBC, NPR, NHK, and more
- Filter by region to quickly find channels
- Search by station name, country, or genre
- Save your favorite channels for quick access
- Background playback — keep listening with the app closed
- Lock screen playback controls
- Korean & English language support
- Clean dark theme UI

Included Channels:
Korea — KBS, MBC, SBS, EBS, YTN, TBS, Military FM, Gugak FM
USA — NPR News, KEXP, WFMU Freeform
UK — BBC Radio 1/2/3/4/6 Music, Classic FM
Japan — NHK Radio 1/2/FM
Europe — France Inter, FIP, Deutschlandfunk, and more
Others — ABC Radio, Triple J, and more
